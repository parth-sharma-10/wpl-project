Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel a booking? +
Cancel via My Bookings up to 30 minutes before your slot with no charge. Late cancellations incur a 50% fee. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
What if a charger is not working? +
Report it using the form above or call our helpline. We respond within 2 hours. You will not be charged for sessions on faulty equipment.
Which connectors are supported? +
Our network supports Type 2 (AC), CCS2 (DC Fast), CHAdeMO, and GB/T connectors. Filter by connector type in the catalogue to find compatible stations.
How is the session fee calculated? +
You are billed per kWh consumed (₹9–18/kWh depending on station), plus a flat ₹5 platform fee and 18% GST. An estimate is shown before you confirm.
Can I extend my charging session? +
Extensions are available if the next slot is unbooked. Request at the station kiosk. Extensions are billed at the standard rate for the additional time.
Is there a mobile app? +
VoltGrid is currently a web application optimised for mobile. A native iOS and Android app is in development and will be available in the next update.
